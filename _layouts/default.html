The risk

Most people think they are sharing an ordinary photo

In reality, a single image can reveal more than intended, and once it is sent, you do not get that exposure back.

License plate

A casual car photo can expose information you never meant to publish

Once it is sent to a group chat, social feed, or marketplace listing, you no longer control where that image travels next.

Location clues

Scenery shots and everyday photos can reveal where you were

What is visible in the frame, combined with EXIF metadata, can tell strangers more about your location than you intended.

QR codes

A screenshot or poster can share access you never meant to pass along

Invite links, tickets, payment codes, or internal group QR codes are easy to overlook and even easier to spread.

Personal details

Names, phone numbers, email addresses, and addresses often hide in plain sight

They may not jump out at first glance, which is exactly why people notice them only after the photo is already gone.

The fix

BeforeShare is not a photo editor. It is a pre-sharing safety layer.

BeforeShare detects privacy risks before you share and gives you quick ways to fix them. No app switching. No new workflow. No sending photos to the cloud.

  • Launches directly from the share sheet, at the exact moment a photo is about to leave your phone
  • Scans for license plates, location clues, QR codes, and personal details before you send
  • Suggests fast fixes in one tap, while still letting you review items individually
