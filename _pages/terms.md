## Description of Service {#description-of-service}

BeforeShare is a photo privacy detection and cleanup tool for iPhone. The app uses on-device technologies to help users:

- scan photos for privacy risks before sharing
- detect faces, text, QR codes, barcodes, and visible details that may require redaction
- blur or redact selected content
- review and remove metadata before exporting a safer copy

BeforeShare is intended to assist with privacy review, not to replace the user's own final review of the image before sharing.
